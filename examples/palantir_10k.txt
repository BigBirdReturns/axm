Palantir Technologies Inc.
Form 10-K Annual Report
Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2024

BUSINESS OVERVIEW

Palantir Technologies Inc. is a software company headquartered in Denver, Colorado. 
The company was founded in 2003 by Peter Thiel, Alex Karp, and others. CEO Alex Karp 
has led the company since its founding.

FINANCIAL HIGHLIGHTS

For fiscal year 2024, we achieved the following results:

Total Revenue: $2.87 billion
- Government Revenue: $1.72 billion (60% of total)
- Commercial Revenue: $1.15 billion (40% of total)

Revenue Growth: 25% year-over-year
- Government Growth: 18% year-over-year
- Commercial Growth: 37% year-over-year

Profitability:
Net Income: $462 million
Operating Margin: 16%
Free Cash Flow: $730 million

GUIDANCE

For fiscal year 2025, we expect:
- Total Revenue: $3.5 billion to $3.7 billion
- Revenue Growth: 22% to 29%
- Operating Margin: 18% to 20%

RISK FACTORS

- We have a history of operating losses prior to 2023
- Significant concentration in government contracts (60% of revenue)
- Intense competition from established technology companies including Microsoft, 
  Amazon Web Services, and Google Cloud
- Reliance on key personnel, particularly CEO Alex Karp
- Geopolitical risks affecting government spending
